Playlist Converter

Convert your Youtube playlists into Spotify playlists

  • 1. Enter a valid playlist url to the search bar
  • 2. Select/deselect songs to export
  • 3. Name the playlist and export

  • Warning: there might be some discrepencies. Please unselect the song if so

  • Note: Please use official song titles for most optimal results

How Does It Work?

Many users use Youtube as a platform to stream music. However, when switching platforms the tedious tasks of finding all your songs and adding them again is time consuming. This tool helps you transfer over your playlists from Youtube to Spotify with ease.
